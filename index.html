Medicinal Mushrooms and Mycotherapy

Medicinal mushrooms are fungi with therapeutic properties that are used to improve people's health, wellbeing, and quality of life.

They have many health benefits supported by clinical trials, scientific studies, and extensive literature by universities and research groups around the world. These studies demonstrate that mushrooms are potent producers of active biomolecules capable of promoting health and wellbeing.
